What Exactly is Encrypted?

We don't just encrypt passwords; we encrypt your entire financial identity:

  • Your Identity: Name, Email, Account Nicknames
  • Your Wealth: Balances, Limits, Available Credit
  • Your Footprint: Transactions, Merchants, Rules
  • Your Documents: Filenames and PDF Passwords
  • Your AI Insights: Raw financial analyses and advisor reports

How Does It Work?

Application-Layer Encryption

Unlike standard databases that only encrypt the hard drive, we encrypt your specific data fields before they are ever saved.

AES-256 Grade Security

We use the same cryptographic algorithms trusted by governments and top-tier financial institutions worldwide.

Salted & Peppered Hashes

We use advanced cryptographic hashing (SHA-256) with unique "peppers" to ensure predictable data cannot be reverse-engineered.

Zero-Knowledge Architecture

Because decryption happens at the application layer when you log in, our database administrators and support staff physically cannot read your sensitive information.
